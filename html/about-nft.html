ABOUT NFTs

NFTs, or non-fungible tokens, are a type of digital asset that has gained significant attention in recent years. Unlike traditional cryptocurrencies such as Bitcoin, which are interchangeable with one another, each NFT is unique and cannot be exchanged on a one-to-one basis.
NFTs are typically created using blockchain technology, which provides a secure and transparent method of storing and verifying ownership of digital assets. This means that an NFT owner can prove that they are the rightful owner of a particular digital asset, whether it be a piece of art, a video, or a tweet.
